Common Questions

How do I connect with my person?

Open Yubi, go to Settings, and share your invite code with your person. Once they enter it, you're connected.

How do I add the widget?

Long press your home screen, tap the + button in the top corner, search for "Yubi," and add the widget. Your person will appear right on your screen.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app under Settings.
